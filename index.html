add match games Our learning platform empowers students to master the probability topic of discrete math through asynchronous learning. We have developed a study tool based on flashcards utilizing the web platform Quizlet. By leveraging Quizlet's quiz mode, students can quiz themselves on a set of cards and progress through levels as they answer more questions correctly. Additionally, we have implemented assessment and gamification features. Our platform includes interactive content and learning management features, offering test mode and study controls. The major advantage of our Quizlet flashcards is the inclusion of numerous games and activities that can be enjoyed while studying. This gamification enhances user enjoyment and motivation. Here's how to use it: students need to study using the Flashcards mode. Open a set, select Flashcards, click anywhere on the card to flip it over, and use the arrow buttons on the keyboard or click the arrows below the card to navigate through the set.
